Frequently Asked Questions

What is Land-Converter?

+ -

Land area converter helps you to convert one unit of measurement to another unit of measurement. In Himachal Pradesh , the land area is measured in different units like kanal, marla ,biswa ,bhiga etc. People find it very difficult to do the inter-conversion between these units as they accurately dont know the aacurrate measurement.

What is Kanal and Marla?

+ -

Kanal and Marla are most prominently used in Himachal Pradesh especially in Kangra,Hamirpur etc. Usually 1 kanal is equal to 20 marlas. But it is still diiferent in different parts of himachal pradesh.

For further information, visit himbhumi.nic.in

What is Bigha and Biswa?

+ -

Bigha and Biswa are both land conversion units .These are most commonly used in shimla and the regions of Solan, sirmaur. The biswa and bigha are measured in different ways in the different regions of Himachal Pradesh. Thus , it is diificult to calculate the accurate result.

For further information, visit himbhumi.nic.in

Some details of Karam ?

+ -

The usage of Square Karam for the measurement of land is prevalent in the States of Himachal Pradesh, Haryana, and Punjab. However, it is a traditional unit for measuring smaller lands, including farmlands, residential lands, and commercial lands in rural areas, usually below one acre. One Karam is equivalent to 5.5 feet.
